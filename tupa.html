Features

Built for the way developers actually work.

No bloat, no telemetry, no popups asking you to upgrade. Just a fast editor that respects your attention and gets sharper the more you use it.

⚡

Native performance

Built in Swift + Rust on Metal, not Electron. Cold start under 0.5s. Open massive repos without hitting the fan.

⌘

Multi-language

First-class Rust, Swift, Python, Go, TypeScript, Zig. LSP everywhere. Tree-sitter highlighting that actually works in mixed-language files.

AI

AI as a peer, not a popup

Sidebar chat that knows your repo. Inline refactors. Code review on commit. Bring your own LLM key — Claude, GPT, local Llama.

⌚

Wearables-aware

Apple Watch / Vision Pro for build status, breakpoint nudges, and pomodoro nags. The IDE follows you to the kitchen.

📐

Designed by a developer

Vim mode, modal commands, no-mouse-required workflows. Configurable to your bone, sane defaults out of the box.

🔒

Local-first, private

No telemetry. Code never leaves your machine unless you point AI at a remote model. Settings sync via your own iCloud, end-to-end encrypted.
